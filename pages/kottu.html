History of Kottu Roti

Kottu Roti is a popular Sri Lankan street food made from shredded roti, vegetables, eggs, and a choice of meat or cheese. Originating as a humble street food, it has grown in popularity and is now served even in high-end restaurants across Sri Lanka.

What is Kottu Roti?

Kottu Roti, also known simply as "Kottu," is a stir-fried dish that combines roti pieces with vegetables, spices, and proteins like chicken, beef, or cheese. It’s typically prepared with a rhythmic chopping sound that’s characteristic of street food vendors in Sri Lanka.

Can I Make Kottu Roti at Home?

Yes, you can! To make Kottu Roti at home, prepare shredded roti or paratha, and combine it with spices, vegetables, and your choice of protein. It's quick and easy to recreate this popular dish in your own kitchen.
