What is Cheese Kottu?

Cheese Kottu is a delicious twist on the traditional Sri Lankan Kottu, blending the rich, creamy goodness of melted cheese with the vibrant flavors of spices and vegetables. It's a perfect dish for cheese lovers looking for a comforting and indulgent meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 large roti or paratha, shredded
  • 200g chicken breast, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

How to Make Cheese Kottu

  1. Heat oil, saut√© garlic and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and spices, cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add veggies, stir-fry for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Add shredded roti, mix well.
  5. Sprinkle cheese, stir until melted.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and lime. Serve hot!

Notes:

For a vegetarian version, skip the chicken and add more vegetables.
