Egg Fried Rice

Egg Fried Rice is a delightful and flavorful dish that combines fluffy rice with scrambled eggs, vibrant vegetables, and a perfect mix of seasonings. This popular dish is stir-fried to achieve a harmonious blend of textures and flavors, where the eggs add a rich, creamy element that complements the savory taste of soy sauce, garlic, and ginger. Ideal for any time of the day, Egg Fried Rice is a versatile and satisfying meal that brings a touch of Asian-inspired cuisine to your table, offering a comforting and delicious experience in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • White Pepper
  • Sesame Oil
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Neutral Oil

How to Make Egg Fried Rice Step by Step

1. Prepare Your Vegetables, Eggs, and Sauce:

Egg Fried Rice comes together quickly, so it’s important to have everything ready before you start cooking.

  1. Chop your vegetables (onions, carrots, peas) and set aside.

  2. Beat your eggs and set aside.



  3. Prepare your soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, and salt and set aside.

2. Sauté The Aromatics:

Heat 2 tablespoons of neutral oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add garlic, onion, and the white parts of scallions. Stir-fry for about 1 minute until fragrant.

3. Cook The Vegetables:

Add chopped carrots and peas to the pan. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.

4. Scramble The Eggs:

Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and add a little more oil if needed. Pour in the beaten eggs and cook until they are scrambled and set, then mix them into the vegetables.

5. Add The Rice and Seasonings:

Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry everything together, adding soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, and salt to taste. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until everything is well mixed and the rice is heated through.

6. Garnish and Serve:

Add the green parts of the scallions and give everything a final stir. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Egg Fried Rice!

Notes:

This recipe serves 2-3 people. You can customize the vegetables and seasonings to your preference.
