Mixed Fried Rice

Mixed Fried Rice is a flavorful and hearty dish that brings together a variety of ingredients like chicken, shrimp, and vegetables, all stir-fried with fragrant rice. The dish is seasoned with a perfect blend of soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil, creating a rich and satisfying meal that's perfect for any occasion. This versatile recipe allows you to enjoy the best of everything in one bowl, offering a delightful mix of textures and flavors that is sure to please everyone.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (sliced)
  • Shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • Eggs
  • Rice (preferably day-old)
  • Onion, Garlic, Scallions
  • Carrots, Peas, Bell Peppers
  • Soy Sauce
  • Shaoxing Wine (optional)
  • White Pepper
  • Sesame Oil
  • Cornstarch (for chicken)
  • Neutral Oil

How to Make Mixed Fried Rice Step by Step

1. Marinate the Chicken:

Slice the chicken into thin pieces. Marinate with soy sauce, white pepper, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set aside for 15-20 minutes.

2. Prepare Your Vegetables, Eggs, and Sauce:

Chop your vegetables (onions, carrots, bell peppers, peas) and set aside. Beat your eggs and set aside. Prepare your soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, and Shaoxing wine (if using) and set aside.

3. Cook the Chicken and Shrimp:

Over high heat, add 2 tbsp of oil. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes until cooked through. Remove and set aside. In the same pan, add the shrimp and cook until pink and opaque, about 2 minutes. Remove and set aside.

4. Saut√© the Aromatics:

If needed, add more oil to the pan. Add garlic, onion, and the white parts of scallions. Stir-fry for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the chopped carrots, bell peppers, and peas; cook for another 2-3 minutes.

5. Cook the Eggs:

Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and add a little more oil if needed. Pour in the beaten eggs and cook until scrambled and set. Mix them into the vegetables.

6. Add Rice and Seasonings:

Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry everything together, adding soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, and Shaoxing wine. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until everything is well mixed and heated through.

7. Combine and Serve:

Add the cooked chicken and shrimp back to the pan. Toss everything together and cook for 1 minute. Garnish with the green parts of the scallions and serve hot.

Notes:

This recipe serves 3-4 people. Feel free to adjust the ingredients according to your preference.
