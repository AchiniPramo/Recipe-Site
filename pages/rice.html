What is Fried Rice?

Fried Rice is a savory dish where rice is stir-fried with a mix of ingredients like eggs, vegetables, and sometimes meats. This meal can be customized to include your favorite ingredients and flavors.

Can I Make Fried Rice at Home?

Absolutely! Fried Rice is quick and easy to make. Start with cold leftover rice, then add your choice of vegetables, eggs, and protein for a delicious homemade version.
