What is Devel?

Devel is a popular Sri Lankan dish known for its spicy and flavorful profile. It features stir-fried meat or vegetables with onions, peppers, and a blend of spices, perfect as a side or main dish.

About Sri Lankan Devel

In Sri Lankan cuisine, "devel" refers to marinated and fried dishes with spices. It can be made with chicken, shrimp, or vegetables like kankung (water spinach) and is known for its spiciness.
