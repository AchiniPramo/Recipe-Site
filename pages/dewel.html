History of Devilled Dishes

Devilled dishes are a staple in Sri Lankan cuisine, famous for their spicy, tangy flavors. They typically feature meat or vegetables marinated and stir-fried with chilies, onions, and spices, creating a delightful balance of heat and savory flavors.

What is Devilled?

"Devilled" dishes are known for their bold flavors, combining spicy, tangy, and slightly sweet notes. The main ingredients are usually chicken, shrimp, or fish, stir-fried with bell peppers, onions, and a special blend of spices that give it a unique taste.

Can I Make Devilled Dishes at Home?

Absolutely! To make devilled dishes, marinate the meat or vegetables with spices, then stir-fry with onions, peppers, and sauces until cooked. Adjust the spiciness to suit your preference.
